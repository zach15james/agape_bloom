Get started

The problem isn't information

Every tool you use stores information. But when you come back to interrupted work after hours, days, or weeks, you waste time mentally reconstructing where you were. The bottleneck isn't what you know — it's re-orienting yourself to what you were doing.

Capture

Text or voice, instantly, even offline. One action. No organizing required.

Re-enter

Hit enter on any context and get a brief — your last thought, open questions, recent thread. All your words.

Context out

Links to your actual tools, files, and folders. Orient complements your stack, never replaces it.

What Orient is not

Not a task manager. Not a calendar. Not a doc editor, AI chat wrapper, or knowledge base you have to maintain.

Orient does one thing: it gets you back to where you were.

Principles

01

Re-entry over reorganization. The goal is resumption, not filing.

02

Your words are hallowed. AI compresses and highlights but never rewrites what you said.

03

Offline-first. Works in bad-signal zones, because that's where people think.

04

One atomic action. Enter or capture. That's it.

05

No engagement traps. No streaks, feeds, or notifications.
