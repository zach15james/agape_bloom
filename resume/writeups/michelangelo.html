Overview

Michelangelo (1991) is a boot sector virus targeting DOS systems. Dormant most of the year, it activates on March 6th — Michelangelo's birthday — overwriting the first 100 sectors of each hard disk and the first 27 sectors of each floppy with garbage, effectively destroying the partition table, FAT, and directory entries.
